Moonspire

The Twilight Gate

Lore

Moonspire sits in a vital and strategic low pass, acting as the "Twilight Gate" between the towering Frostpeak Mountains and the ancient, sprawling White Lion Woods. The city is named for the way the moonlight reflects off the exposed veins of Star-Metal in the mountain pass, bathing the city in a perpetual, silvery twilight.

History & Governance: Founded as a joint outpost by miners and rangers, the city is now governed by a tense coalition. Power is split between the Artisan's Concord (representing the miners and smiths) and the Ranger's Conclave (representing the trappers, lumberjacks, and hunters of the Woods).

Geography & Economy: The city's economy is torn in two. One half, centered in the Stonehold district, is dedicated to mining and forging the supernaturally light and durable Star-Metal. The other half, based in the Woodswatch district, thrives on the timber, furs, and rare herbs from the White Lion Woods.

Culture & Factions: The city is culturally divided. The "Stoners" (miners) are industrious, pragmatic, and insular. The "Woodsfolk" (rangers) are self-sufficient, superstitious, and fiercely protective of the forest. The primary source of conflict is the Star-Metal Madness, a debilitating paranoia that afflicts the miners, which the Woodsfolk fear will "poison" the forest.

City Districts

Stonehold

The main district, built of heavy stone and set against the mountain's base. This is the heart of the Artisan's Concord, containing the great Anvilheart forges, the city's main market, and the residences of the smiths and merchants. The air is warm, smoky, and rings with the sound of hammers.

The Deepvein

The network of mineshafts and crude living quarters dug directly into the mountain, running beneath Stonehold. This is where the raw Star-Metal is harvested. The district is cramped, damp, and lit by the eerie, pulsing glow of the ore. This is the epicenter of the Star-Metal Madness, and the Concord Guard rarely patrols here.

Woodswatch

The sprawling, rustic district on the opposite side of the pass, where the city blurs into the edge of the White Lion Woods. Composed of timber longhouses and tanneries, this is the domain of the Ranger's Conclave. It's home to the trappers, hunters, and lumber mills that feed the city's other economy.

Key NPCs

Guild-King Thorgath Stonehand — The powerful and ambitious leader of the Artisan's Concord. He sees the White Lion Woods as an untapped resource and wants to clear-cut it to expand the mining operations. He dismisses the Madness as a "weakness of the spirit."
First Ranger Briar — The grim, no-nonsense leader of the Ranger's Conclave. She is fiercely protective of the White Lion Woods and believes the Star-Metal is a "blight" that is poisoning the mountain. She advocates for sealing the Deepvein... permanently.
Elara Silversky — An independent alchemist and scholar, formerly of the Artisan's Concord, who now lives in Woodswatch. She is obsessed with finding a mundane cure for the Star-Metal Madness, which she believes is caused by a toxic, breathable vapor from the raw ore.
